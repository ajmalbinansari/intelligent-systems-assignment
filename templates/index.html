Plant Disease Detection

Welcome to the Plant Disease Detection application! This tool helps identify diseases in plant leaves using advanced AI technology.

Simply upload a clear image of a plant leaf, and the system will analyze it to detect potential diseases and provide recommendations. The system currently supports multiple plant types including tomato, potato, apple, corn, grape, cherry, and more.

Get Started

Quick Start Guide
  1. Click on the 'Detect Disease' tab above
  2. Upload a plant leaf photo
  3. The system will automatically analyze the image
  4. View the results and recommendations
